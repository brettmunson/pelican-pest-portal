🛡 TERMITE PREVENTION TIPS Auto-included
Maintain Foundation Visibility

Keep the bottom portion of the foundation visible. Avoid covering with soil, mulch, gravel, or dense vegetation.

Keep Wood Away from Soil

Avoid wood contacting the ground — siding, fence posts, deck supports, landscape timbers, and stored lumber.

Control Moisture Around the Home

Repair leaks promptly, ensure gutters function properly, direct water away from the foundation.

Store Items Away from the Foundation

Do not store firewood, cardboard, or lumber against the home. Maintain clear access for inspection.

How Subterranean Termites Enter Homes

Through foundation cracks, expansion joints, plumbing penetrations, and wood in contact with the ground.
